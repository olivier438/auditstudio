Security Policy

ONE CIRCLE IT SOLUTIONS is committed to the security of Risk Studio and Audit Studio. This page documents our vulnerability disclosure policy and security contact information.

🔐

Report a Vulnerability

Responsible disclosure contact for Risk Studio and Audit Studio

Security Contact
security@onecircle.be
✉ Send Report

We accept reports in English, French, or Dutch. Please include the product name, affected version, description of the issue, and steps to reproduce if available. PGP encryption available on request.

🕐

Coordinated Disclosure Process

Our commitment to responsible vulnerability handling

1
Report Received
Submit your report to security@onecircle.be with description and reproduction steps.
Day 0
2
Acknowledgement
We acknowledge receipt and confirm we are investigating the report.
Within 5 business days
3
Assessment
We assess severity (CVSS v3.1), impact, and affected components. We keep you informed of progress.
Within 15 days
4
Remediation
We develop, test, and deploy a fix. Critical issues patched within 7 days of confirmation.
7 to 90 days depending on severity
5
Coordinated Disclosure
After remediation, we coordinate public disclosure with you. We credit reporters who wish to be acknowledged.
Max 90-day embargo
ONE CIRCLE IT SOLUTIONS will not pursue legal action against researchers who report vulnerabilities in good faith and follow this coordinated disclosure process.
⚠

Severity Classification & Response Targets

Based on CVSS v3.1 scoring

Critical
Remote code execution, full data breach, authentication bypass. CVSS 9.0+
Patch within 7 days
High
Privilege escalation, significant data exposure. CVSS 7.0-8.9
Patch within 30 days
Medium
Limited data exposure, functionality abuse. CVSS 4.0-6.9
Patch within 90 days
Low
Minor information disclosure, low-impact issues. CVSS 0.1-3.9
Patch within 180 days

Regulatory Notification

CRA Article 14 - National CSIRT notification process

In accordance with CRA Article 14, ONE CIRCLE IT SOLUTIONS notifies the Belgian national CSIRT within 24 hours of becoming aware of any actively exploited vulnerability in our products.
